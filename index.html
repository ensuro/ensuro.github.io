Liquidity Providers

Ensuro collects money from different liquidity providers, both retail and institutional, through a Liquidity Pool governed by smart contracts. This capital, collected in the form of cryptographic stablecoins, provides underwriting capacity for Insurtech companies operating in the parametric insurance space.

To constantly monitor the performance of the risk models, Ensuro leverages a Quality Assurance System that monitors whether the price of the policies fall into standard parameters defined at the moment of the integration.
