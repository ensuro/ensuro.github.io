OUR PURPOSE

Democratize insurance and facilitate insurance innovation_


Our Vision_

Our vision is to create a simple entry point to insurance investment for individual investors and a more efficient alternative to traditional (re)insurance companies for risk takers seeking capacity.

Our Mission_

Our mission is to make insurance more accessible to the general public.
We use smart contracts to curate competitive insurance portfolios. We allow anyone to invest in insurance risk and reap its benefits.
