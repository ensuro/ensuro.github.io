OUR PURPOSE

Democratize insurance and facilitate insurance innovation._

Our vision_

Our vision is to create a simple entry point to insurance investment for individual investors and a more efficient alternative to traditional (re)insurance companies for risk takers seeking capacity.

Our Mision_

Our mission is to make insurance more accessible to the general public. We will do this by (1) using innovative technology to provide the opportunity for anyone to invest in insurance risk and benefit from its lucrative returns and (2) partnering with risk-takers and not-for-profit entities to provide insurance capacity in a more innovative and efficient manner.
