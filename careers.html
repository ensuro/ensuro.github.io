We use smart contracts to curate competitive insurance portfolios. We allow anyone to invest in insurance risk and reap its benefits. By opening up the insurance market to new players, we fuel innovation for established and upcoming insurance partners.






We are a fully remote global team, we believe in working hard and playing hard, but most importantly, we are passionate about making investment in insurance more accessible to the general public. We want to democratize insurance and facilitate insurance innovation.

OUR VALUES

United by our values_

We always listen

We strive to exercise empathy to understand and approach the desires, needs, difficulties and work of those who trust in our proposal. We know that listening is the essential basis for achieving relevance.

No one is left out

We are based on merits and achievements and give everyone equal opportunities.

Creative DNA has no hierarchies

We believe in the power of each person to do great things. We know that the best ideas come from the synergy achieved through teamwork. We promote and create spaces for the individual development of each person we work with.

We dream and we create

We devise and create the future of insurance. For this, we design and develop ambitious products that bet on great ideas, iteration and constant improvement. We value effort and learning, which helps us to constantly surpass ourselves and encourages us to challenge ourselves more and more.

Working at Ensuro_

The hiring process behind Ensuro
We are looking for talented and passionate people to help Ensuro achieve its mission. Our goal is to conduct an efficient yet thorough hiring process that allows us to get to know you beyond your resume and will enable you to ask us all the questions you might have about Ensuro.

Learning and development

Competitive compensation

Flexible working hours

Fully remote

International and multicultural team

Company Equity

STAGE 01

Meet the Hiring Manager

The first interview will be a video call of 30-45 min.

STAGE 02

Ensuro challenge

The second stage will be a meeting of about 60 minutes, where we will propose a task to be defined before the session for you to present it.

STAGE 03

Fit interview

The third stage is a 60-minute virtual meeting that will allow you to get to know Ensuro team members and founders. This conversation is an opportunity to get to know each other better and clarify any doubts either party may have.
